Stephen Hawking The Dreamer

Genius personified

Stephen Hawking developed the idea of singularity, and in his extensive career continued to question the universe and expand our idea of existence itself

Personal Facts

  • Born: January 8th, 1942
  • Origin: Oxford, United Kingdom
  • Education: Cambridge
  • Best Known for: Author of the bestselling book, "A Brief History of Time", as well as many theories and hypothesis on the mysteries of the universe
  • Fun Fact: After contracting motor-neurone disease at age 21 and being given only two years to live, he went on to live another 56 years and become the Lucasian Professor of Mathematics at the University of Cambridge, the post held by Isaac Newton.
