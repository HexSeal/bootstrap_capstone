Elon Musk The Mad Scientist

A Legend in the Making

Still in the height of his career, Elon Musk continues to make headlines and wow shareholders with his creativity, childish mischeviousness and inherent brilliance.

Personal Facts

  • Born: June 28, 1971
  • Origin: Pretoria, South Africa
  • Education: University of Pennsylvania
  • Best Known for: Founder, CEO and chief engineer of SpaceX, Founder and CEO of The Boring Company, CEO of Tesla Inc.
  • Fun Fact: Elon Musk has many side-projects, including Starlink, a system of satellites that he hopes will one day bring wireless connection to even the most remote places on Earth.
